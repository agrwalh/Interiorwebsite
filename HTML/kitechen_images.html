Kitchen Project

Photo Gallery

The project aimed to utilize the provided kitchen layout. Due to the rectangular shape of the space, I opted for a "U" shaped kitchen layout. This furniture arrangement allowed for the creation of a cooking zone and a dining area.
I used light-colored furniture, mainly in white, with accents of light wood on the countertops. The floor featured glazed porcelain Calacatta Silver tiles, giving the interior an elegant character. To enrich the design, I used deep bottle green on the walls.
